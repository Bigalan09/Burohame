    How to play

    1. Place every piece in the rack on the 9×9 board.
    2. Fill rows, columns, or 3×3 boxes to clear them.
    3. Keep space open so future racks still fit.

    Helpful reminders

    • Coach Mode spots risky holes and safer lanes.
    • Combos matter, but board shape matters more.
    • Shop colourways and finishes are cosmetic only, so score still comes first.
